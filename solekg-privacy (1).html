SolEKG is developed and maintained by Sascha. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the SolEKG app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

SolEKG does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

SolEKG fetches public cryptocurrency data from CoinGecko API and market sentiment from Alternative.me. These requests are made directly from your device. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Since we collect no data, this App is safe for all ages.
